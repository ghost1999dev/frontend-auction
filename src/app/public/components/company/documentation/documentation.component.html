Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on "Sign Up" and choose whether you're a company or developer. Fill in your details and start using CodeBid.
What is a reverse auction?
In a reverse auction, developers compete by offering the lowest price for a project posted by a company.
Is CodeBid free to use?
Yes, signing up and posting projects is free. We charge a small fee on completed transactions.
How does blockchain ensure security?
Blockchain provides a transparent and tamper-proof record of all transactions, ensuring trust and reliability.
